# 🚀 Career Copilot

AI-powered career assistant that helps professionals discover better career opportunities, understand their market fit, and create a personalized roadmap to reach their goals.

## 🎯 Project Purpose

Career Copilot is a SaaS platform designed to bridge the gap between a person's current professional profile and their next career opportunity.

The platform analyzes a user's resume, extracts skills, experience, and career signals, then matches that profile against relevant job opportunities using AI-powered semantic analysis.

Beyond job matching, Career Copilot helps users understand:

- Where they currently stand in the market
- Which opportunities best fit their profile
- Why a specific role matches their experience
- Which skills they need to develop to reach their desired career path

The goal is to transform job searching from a passive application process into an intelligent career development journey.

---

# 💡 Core Features (MVP)

## Resume Intelligence

Users upload their resume and Career Copilot:

- Extracts professional experience
- Identifies technical and soft skills
- Creates a structured career profile
- Generates semantic embeddings for intelligent matching

---

## AI Job Matching

The system compares the user's profile against job opportunities and provides:

- Match percentage score
- Matching skills
- Missing skills
- Experience alignment
- Career fit explanation
